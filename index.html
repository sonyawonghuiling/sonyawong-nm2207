About

SPACE, like its namesake, is a data story on the spatial availability in Singapore. I was inspired by my own worries about finding stability and security in Singapore after I graduate from university. As a young undergraduate concerned about my place in Singapore in the future, I believe that many of my peers carry the same concerns as I do. This led me to think about the scarcity in Singapore â€“ how we have little land and resources, yet our population growth is unprecendented, bordering on a problem of overcrowding. Therefore, I hope that this data story will be able to give a more in-depth glimpse into an illustration of current population statistics and make logical predictions about what may come in the near future. I seek to answer the big question, "How much space is there left in Singapore?" through looking at population indicators.
